Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Public API Finder ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit public-api-finder.vercel.app (the "Site"), how that information is used, and what rights you have with respect to that information.

Public API Finder is a read-only API directory tool. We do not offer user accounts, subscriptions, or payment services. This fundamental design choice means the personal data we are capable of collecting is extremely limited. We collect only what is necessary to operate the site and display the advertising that funds our free service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of data during your use of the Site:

  • Anonymous Visit Data: When you load a page, we log a timestamp, the page URL, your browser's user-agent string, and your approximate timezone. This data does not contain your IP address in personally identifiable form and is used only to maintain a visitor counter displayed on the site and understand aggregate usage patterns.
  • Anonymized Search Queries: Search terms entered into the API search bar may be logged anonymously to help us understand which API categories and types are most in demand, guiding future curation decisions. No personal information is associated with these search logs.
  • Click-Through Data: We may record which APIs are clicked (by API name only) to understand which listings are most popular and most useful to our visitors. No personal identifying information is attached to these records.
  • Language Preference: Your selected interface language is stored in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

3. No User Accounts

Public API Finder does not offer user registration or account creation of any kind. We do not collect passwords, personal names, home addresses, phone numbers, payment information, or any other personally identifying information as part of normal site operation. Your favorites list is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage — it is never transmitted to any server and is not accessible to us. This account-free design is intentional: by not collecting personal data, we cannot lose it, misuse it, or fail to protect it.

4. Browser Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage (not cookies) to store the following data on your device:

  • Favorites: APIs you have saved as favorites are stored in localStorage and persist across browser sessions on the same device. This data is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Language Preference: Your selected UI language is saved so you do not need to re-select it on each visit.
  • Anonymous Visit Statistics: A local counter value used to display daily and total visitor counts on the site.

You can clear all localStorage data at any time through your browser's developer tools (Application tab > Local Storage) or by clearing your browser's site data in settings. Clearing this data will remove your saved favorites and reset your language preference.

5. Cookies and Advertising

Public API Finder does not set any first-party cookies. However, we display advertisements through two third-party networks, both of which may set cookies in your browser:

  • Google AdSense: Google uses the DoubleClick cookie and related technologies to serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing history across websites that use Google's advertising services. You may opt out of personalized ads from Google by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Google's Privacy Policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Adsterra: Adsterra may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to deliver, target, and measure advertising performance. You may manage interest-based advertising preferences through the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page or the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out page.

Advertising revenue from these networks is what allows us to offer Public API Finder as a completely free service. We are transparent about this relationship and encourage you to use the opt-out options above if you prefer not to receive personalized advertising.

6. Third-Party Services

In addition to advertising networks, we use the following services:

  • Vercel: Our site is hosted on Vercel's infrastructure. Vercel may collect standard server logs (including anonymized IP addresses) as part of their hosting service. Vercel's privacy policy governs that data.
  • Google Sheets: We use a Google Sheets webhook to store anonymous visit log data. This data contains no personally identifiable information. Google's privacy policy applies to data stored in Google's systems.
  • Tailwind CSS CDN: We load the Tailwind CSS framework from a public CDN. The CDN provider may log requests including your IP address as part of standard CDN operations.

7. GDPR Rights (EU and EEA Residents)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) gives you rights including: the right to access personal data we hold about you; the right to rectification of inaccurate data; the right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"); the right to restrict or object to processing; and the right to data portability.

Because Public API Finder does not collect personally identifiable information from standard visitors, most of these rights are moot for typical site use. If you have concerns about data collected by our third-party advertising partners (Google, Adsterra), those are addressed through each partner's own GDPR compliance mechanisms and opt-out tools. For any other GDPR inquiry, please contact us at taeshinkim11@gmail.com.

8. CCPA Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to request deletion of personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. Public API Finder does not sell personal information. We do not collect personal information from standard site visitors beyond what is described in this policy. To exercise any CCPA right, please contact us at taeshinkim11@gmail.com.

9. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the limited data we collect. All communications with the Site are encrypted via HTTPS. Anonymous analytics data stored in Google Sheets is protected by Google account security. We do not store sensitive personal information, which means the risk of meaningful harm from a data breach is minimal. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

Public API Finder is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If a parent or guardian believes their child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at taeshinkim11@gmail.com and we will delete that information immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Public API Finder after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy, please contact us at taeshinkim11@gmail.com. We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 5 business days.
